UCC-ORCHESTRATOR-INTERNALS
==========================

This document defines internal orchestrator requirements.
It is supporting, not normative.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. INTERNAL REQUIREMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------

The orchestrator MUST:
- log every state transition
- assert valid workflow structure before execution begins
- detect and reject invalid dependency graphs
- crash fast on internal inconsistency
- be fully testable in isolation

Internal bugs are categorically not workflow failures.

------------------------------------------------------------

2. INTERNAL LOGIC
------------------------------------------------------------

The orchestrator MUST NOT contain business logic.

------------------------------------------------------------

END OF DOCUMENT
