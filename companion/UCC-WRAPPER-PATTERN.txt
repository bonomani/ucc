UCC-WRAPPER-PATTERN
===================

This document describes the wrapper pattern around UCC execution.
It is non-authoritative.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The wrapper pattern separates:
- orchestration
- observation
- declaration
- diff
- transition execution
- reporting

------------------------------------------------------------

2. RESPONSIBILITY BOUNDARY
------------------------------------------------------------

The wrapper may:
- call UCC primitives
- route inputs
- collect diagnostics

The wrapper must not:
- redefine UCC semantics
- alter the closed result model
- hide failures

------------------------------------------------------------

END OF DOCUMENT
