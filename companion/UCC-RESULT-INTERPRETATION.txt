UCC-STATUS – Consumer Interpretation
====================================

This document is NORMATIVE.
It defines how consumers interpret UCC result states.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. CONSUMER RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- treat observation=ok, outcome=converged as success
- treat observation=ok, outcome=changed as success
- treat observation=ok, outcome=unchanged as deferred or inhibited
- treat observation=ok, outcome=failed as failure
- treat observation=failed as observation infrastructure failure
- treat observation=indeterminate as ambiguous target state
