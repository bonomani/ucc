BFO-CLI – Observability
=======================

This document defines the observability surface of the MVP.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. OBSERVABILITY
------------------------------------------------------------

Observability is CLI-visible and file-logged.

Events:
- workflow_started
- step_started
- step_completed
- step_failed
- workflow_converged

All events are:
- structured
- timestamped
- GIC by nature
